Auto Skip for Prime and Netflix (ASPN)

You have done all the hard work, sit back relax and binge-watch. This extension starts working as soon as you install it and works both for Prime and Netflix. What does it do for you?

  • Auto Skips intros and recaps on both Prime and Netflix
  • Auto Skips credits and play next episode on Prime
  • Auto Skips Ads on Prime

NOTE: Please refresh the Netflix and Prime page once after you install it.
